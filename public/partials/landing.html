PLAN HEALTHY MEALS WITH EASE

Macronutrients are the main component of a balanced diet. Our bodies require larger amounts of macronutrients than micronutrients like vitamins. The three macronutrients are: carbohydrates, protein, and fat. Each performs vital functions in the body. Make sure you're reaching healthy nutritional goals with the Meet Your Macros app.

    Daily Food Group Requirements

  • Grains: 6-11 Servings
  • Fruits: 2-4 Servings
  • Vegetables: 3-5 Servings
  • Protein: 2-3 Servings
  • Dairy: 2-3 Servings
  • Fats: Use Sparingly

    Macronutrient Ratio Goals

  • Carbs: 50%
  • Protein: 30%
  • Fats: 20%

The 50/30/20 ratio provides a healthy balance of macronutrients in your diet. However, not every body type is the same, and different people with different body types (especially if you're exercising regularly) may want to fine tune macronutrient ratios.

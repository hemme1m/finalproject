What are Macronutrients?

Macronutrients are the main component of a balanced diet. Our bodies require larger amounts of macronutrients than micronutrients, like vitamins. The three macronutrients are: protein, fat, and carbohydrates. Each perform vital functions in the body. Make sure you're eating enough of each with the Meet Your Macros app.

    Macronutrient Requirements
  • Protein: 50% of calories
  • Carbohydrates: 30% of calories
  • Fats: 20% of calories
